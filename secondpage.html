At Preferred Laundry & Dry Cleaning, we treat every item with care from start to finish:

  • Check & Sort We inspect your clothes and sort them by fabric and color.
  • Stain Removal Tough stains are pre-treated gently and effectively.
  • Wash or Dry Clean We use the right method for each item, whether it’s a machine wash or delicate dry cleaning.
  • Dry & Press Clothes are carefully dried, then ironed or steamed for a crisp finish.
  • Final Check Everything is reviewed to make sure it’s clean, fresh, and ready for pickup.
